Welcome!

Oddwords is a quasi-journalistic site founded in 2018, created to document and discuss the various games and events related to the Oddworld series, its community, and its developers. While most of the content on the site deals with Soulstorm, especially its pre-release material, you can also find game critiques, interviews, articles about various more or less known aspects of the series, a few surveys conducted by me, and even a couple of fanfics. I hope you enjoy your stay!
